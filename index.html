About Us :

LearnLoom is an innovative and dynamic platform that bridges the gap between educators and learners, offering a seamless experience for sharing and accessing study resources. Designed with a modern and responsive interface, this website empowers providers to upload essential materials such as notes, guides, and links with ease, while seekers gain convenient access to these resources in a well-organized format.


LearnLoom is more than a website; it’s a hub of innovation where technology meets education. By connecting those who have knowledge to share with those eager to learn, it transforms traditional resource sharing into a creative and engaging experience. LearnLoom truly embodies the spirit of collaborative learning and growth.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is LearnLoom?

LearnLoom is an online platform designed to help educators (providers) upload study materials and share resources with students (seekers) looking for academic support.

How can I become a provider?

To become a provider, simply sign up on our website, complete your profile, and start uploading your study materials for seekers to access.

How can seekers access the study materials?

Seekers can log in to their accounts, browse available resources uploaded by providers, and download or view materials that are relevant to their studies.

Is LearnLoom free to use?

Yes! LearnLoom is completely free for both providers and seekers. We believe in providing accessible educational resources for everyone.

Can I upload any type of material?

Providers can upload a wide range of study materials, including PDFs, PowerPoint presentations, notes, and useful links. However, we encourage the upload of educational, relevant, and respectful content.
